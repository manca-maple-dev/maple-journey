# Publishing Notes

These files are deployment-ready templates, not final legal advice.

## Files Included

- PRIVACY_POLICY.md
- TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md
- COOKIE_POLICY.md
- AI_AND_LEGAL_DISCLAIMER.md
- DEPLOYMENT_LEGAL_CHECKLIST.md

## What You Must Fill In Before Publishing

- company legal name
- business address
- support email
- privacy email
- legal email
- governing law and venue
- live vendor list
- actual retention periods
- actual refund and cancellation rules
- actual messaging and consent practices
- effective date / last updated date

## Recommended Public Footer Links

- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Cookie Policy
- Legal Disclaimer
- Contact / Support

## Strong Recommendation

Have a licensed lawyer review the final text before public launch, especially if you will:

- charge users money
- process refugee or immigration-related personal data
- send proactive notifications
- store sensitive identifiers
- market the service as legal or regulated support
