# Deployment Legal Checklist

This checklist is for final pre-launch review. It is not legal advice.

## Company Identity

- [ ] Confirm legal entity name
- [ ] Confirm business address
- [ ] Confirm privacy contact email
- [ ] Confirm legal/support contact email
- [ ] Confirm governing law jurisdiction
- [ ] Confirm whether MapleJourney is operating as a corporation, sole proprietorship, or other entity

## Product Claims

- [ ] Remove any claim that MapleJourney provides legal advice unless that is actually licensed and documented
- [ ] Remove any claim of guaranteed outcomes
- [ ] Verify all references to government or third-party sources are accurate and non-misleading
- [ ] Verify subscription tiers, prices, and refund rules match the live product
- [ ] Verify whether WhatsApp, SMS, and other messaging channels are actually enabled at launch

## Privacy and Data Mapping

- [ ] Confirm exactly what user data is collected
- [ ] Confirm what data is optional versus required
- [ ] Confirm retention periods for accounts, logs, chats, reminders, and deleted accounts
- [ ] Confirm whether deleted accounts are hard deleted, soft deleted, or retained for legal/security reasons
- [ ] Confirm all vendors that process personal information
- [ ] Confirm where data is hosted and whether data leaves Canada
- [ ] Confirm whether analytics tools or ad trackers are active

## Security and Access

- [ ] Confirm access control for admin features
- [ ] Confirm authentication and password reset flows
- [ ] Confirm secrets are not committed and production keys are rotated
- [ ] Confirm logging does not expose sensitive data
- [ ] Confirm incident response contact and process

## Payments

- [ ] Confirm payment processor terms and checkout flow
- [ ] Confirm whether taxes are charged
- [ ] Confirm cancellation flow
- [ ] Confirm refund and chargeback handling

## Messaging and Notifications

- [ ] Confirm phone consent wording if SMS or WhatsApp is enabled
- [ ] Confirm opt-out mechanism for non-essential messages
- [ ] Confirm message-rate disclosures if needed
- [ ] Confirm notification preferences are respected

## Documents To Publish

- [ ] Privacy Policy
- [ ] Terms of Service
- [ ] Cookie Policy
- [ ] AI and Legal Disclaimer
- [ ] Support / contact page
- [ ] Data deletion / privacy request instructions

## Final Review

- [ ] Counsel or qualified advisor review completed
- [ ] Internal sign-off completed
- [ ] Publication dates updated in all legal documents
- [ ] Public URLs decided for all legal documents
