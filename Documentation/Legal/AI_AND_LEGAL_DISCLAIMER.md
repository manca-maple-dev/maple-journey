Use of MapleJourney does not create:

- a lawyer-client relationship
- an immigration consultant-client relationship
- a representative relationship
- a fiduciary relationship
- an emergency or crisis support service relationship

If you need legal representation, regulated immigration advice, emergency housing, urgent medical help, or crisis support, contact qualified professionals or emergency services directly.
